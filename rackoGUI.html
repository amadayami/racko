How to Play Racko

Object

The first player to score 500 points wins! You win a hand by arranging your cards from low to high and getting a Racko!

Set-Up

  1. MODE: Choose between standard and double Racko.

    Standard Racko is played with 60 cards (1-60) in a deck and 10 cards in a player's hand.

    Double Racko is played with 120 cards (1-120) in a deck and 20 cards in a player's hand.

  2. PLAYERS: Choose the number of players (4 players max). You can also choose to play against computer players!

    Note: You must play with at least one human.

Gameplay

ON YOUR TURN: You pick one card, the top of either the draw or the discard pile, and place it in your rack.

If you take from the DISCARD pile, you must replace a card from your rack with the card you drew. The card you replace is then placed on the top of the discard pile.

If you take from the DRAW pile, you can either replace a card from your rack with the card you drew, or place in on the top of the discard pile.

Gameplay continues with players alternating turns until one player gets a Racko. The player who gets Racko receives 75 points and all other players receive 5 points for each card they have in ascending order from the bottom.

Winning the Game

Continue playing rounds until a player scores 500 points. Note: If two players score more than 500 points, then the highest total wins!
